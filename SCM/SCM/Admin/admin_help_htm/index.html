S. No FAQ

1. How can I change my password?

The application password can be changed at any time by clicking on the ‘Change Password’ hyperlink in the top right corner user icon. Enter the existing and new password, and click 'Submit' to save changes.

2. How do I view the number of paid/unpaid bills?

On the Dashboard, in the ‘Customer Engagement Analytics’ module, click on the ‘Billing’ tab to view details of paid and unpaid bills. The information can be viewed in list and graph format.

3. Where can all notifications received be viewed?

On the Dashboard, in the ‘Customer Engagement Analytics’ module, all notifications received by the utility can be viewed under the ‘Notifications’ tab. Categories displayed include: 'Service Requests’ and 'Billing Notifications,' and can be viewed in list and graph format.

4. What information does the ‘Customer Behavior’ section provide?

On the Dashboard, in the Analytics module, click the 'Customer Behavior' tab. This section captures the customer information, such as online payment, device used, screen resolution, browser used, and login details.

5. Where do I find registered/active/inactive customers?

On the Dashboard, in the CSR Workbench module, click the ‘Customers' tab. Then click the pie chart icon to view data on registered/active/inactive customers. Data can also be viewed in list and graph format.

6. How do I find how many customers are enrolled/un-enrolled for paperless billing?

On the Dashboard, in the ‘Customer Engagement Analytics’ module, click the ‘Paperless Billing’ tab to view the number of customers in list and graph format.

7. Where can I find reports for Push Notifications?

In the Analytics module, click the ‘Push Notification’ tab to view a report/graph on pending, delivered and failed messages out of the total number of messages sent. You can also export this report into pdf or excel format.

8. How do I view the number of customers enrolled in Marketing Preferences being offered?

In the Analytics module, click on the ‘Marketing Preference’ tab to view the number of customers enrolled in each offer (i.e. News Releases, Service Offerings, Newsletters, Energy Saving Toolkits, Cool Tips Brochures, Community Benefit Programs).

9. Can I get a report on the number of clicks on banners in the customer portal?

Yes, the report on the number of banner clicks can be fetched from the ‘Banners’ tab in Analytics.

10. Can the reports be downloaded?

Yes, the reports can be downloaded in Excel or PDF format. In the top right corner, click the ‘Export’ hyperlink, provided in each section, to download the report in the selected format.

11. Where do I view the messages received by the utility?

On the Dashboard, the ‘Notification’ module contains all the messages received by the utility from its customers. The categories displayed include: 'Billing,' 'Services,' 'Connect Me,' and ‘Login Issues’.

12. How do I configure the settings for Auto response mails?

On the Dashboard, in the ‘Notification’ module, click the ‘Auto Response’ tab, enter the message, and click ‘Save'.

13. How do I send Email or Push notifications to customers?

On the Dashboard, in the ‘Notification’ module, click the ‘Send Notification’ tab. Enter the required information and click ‘Send’. The message will be sent to the selected customer(s) and reflect in their Town of Braselton inbox.

14. How do I add a user in the Customer Service Portal?

On the Dashboard, click on the Administration module, click on the ‘Users’ tab. Click ‘Add User', enter the required information, and click save to add a new user.

15. How do I edit users and change the status for a user?

On the Dashboard, in the Administration module, navigate to the 'Users' tab. To edit a user, click ’Edit’ icon, adjust details, and click save to update the changes.

To change the status for any user from active to inactive or vice-versa, click on the ‘Active/Inactive’ tab.

16. How do I add a Payment Location?

On the Dashboard, in the Administration module, click the 'Payment Locations’ tab. Click ‘Add Payment Location,’ enter the required information, and click ‘Save’. The added payment location will reflect on the customer portal, as well as, in the list on the customer service portal.

17. Can I configure the email templates?

Yes. On the Dashboard, in the ‘Administration module,’ click the ‘Email Template’ tab. Select a template, enter the required information and click save to update the changes.

18. How do I add a new service type to the Service module?

In the Administration module, click on the ‘Services’ tab. To add a new service type, click on ‘Add Service Type’ and enter the Service name. You can add a representative image by clicking ‘Choose File.’ Click ‘Add’ to save the new service type.

19. How do I define a Notification Template?

In the Administration module, click on the ‘Notification Template’ tab. Select the template and mode of message, write the respective message for mode of message and click ‘Save’. The saved template shall reflect under ‘Send Notification’ on selecting corresponding template in Notification module.

20. How can I add/update the FAQs on customer portal?

In the Administration module, click on the ‘FAQ’ tab. Click ‘Add FAQ’ and enter the required information to add a new question. To edit, click on the ‘Edit’ icon and make changes to the existing question, click ‘Update’ to save the changes. The changes will reflect on customer portal FAQs.

21. How can I configure the ad banners on the customer portal?

In the Administration module, click on the ‘Banners’ tab. Click on ‘Edit’ and make the required changes. Once the changes have been made, click on ‘Update’ to save the changes. The changes will reflect on customer portal.

22. Can I update the Privacy Policy information and Terms & Conditions?

Yes, Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions can be updated in the ‘Administration’ module, under the ‘Privacy Policy’ tab. Update the information and click on “Save’, the changes will reflect in the customer portal.

23. Can I change the disclaimers on the customer portal?

Yes, the disclaimers reflecting in the customer portal can be updated through ‘Administration’ module, under the ‘Disclaimer Settings’ tab. Make the changes and click on ‘Save’, the changes will reflect in the customer portal.

24. How to configure application downtime?

In the Administration module, click on the ‘Schedule Maintenance’ tab. Select the date, enter time at which the application will proceed and determine the duration for downtime. At the start of the scheduled maintenance, the application will not be accessible to the customers.

25. How do I add a new topic to the Connect Me topic list?

On the Dashboard, in the Administration module, click ‘Connect Me.’ Click the ‘Add Topic’ hyperlink, enter the required information, and click save. The newly added topic will reflect in the topic list of the 'Connect Me' module, the customer portal, and the admin portal.

26. How do I update the [Utility Provider] information?

On the Dashboard, in the Administration module, click the ‘Utility Information’ tab. Enter the information as required and click ’Save’.

27. Can the mobile dashboard view be changed?

Yes, the mobile dashboard view can be changed from half view to full view or vice versa under the ‘Mobile Dashboard Settings’ tab. Select the preferences and click ‘Save’. The changes made shall reflect on mobile app.

28. How do I define the security settings for the application?

On the Dashboard, in the Administration module, click the ‘Security Configuration’ tab. Make the changes as required, for previous password count, IP Block, Account Block and lock-out periods and save the changes. The changes will reflect in the customer portal.

29. How do I add new Customers?

On the Dashboard, in the CSR Workbench module, click ‘Add Customer’. Enter the customer details and submit the form. Once the form has been submitted, the customer will receive an email to activate the account.

30. Can I lock/unlock the customers?

Yes, on the Dashboard, in the CSR Workbench module, in the 'Customers’ tab, click the lock/unlock icon to adjust customer account.

31. What additional information is available in customer accounts?

On the Dashboard, in the CSR Workbench module under the 'Customers’ tab, click on a city name to access specific customers. Click on the customer name link to access the information related to the customer's account. The following data can be accessed:

  • Profile
  • Property
  • Notifications
  • Billing/Payments
  • Connect Me
  • Service Requests

32. How can I Login to the customer portal?

On the Dashboard, in the CSR Workbench module under the ‘Customers' tab, click on customer name. Click on the ‘Login to Customer Portal’ button to access the respective customer portal. Note: Logging into a specific customer portal is only accessible if the customer is a registered user.

33. Where can I view the report of customers whose IP address has been blocked?

On the Dashboard, in the CSR Workbench module, click on ‘Blocked IP’ to view the report. The information displayed includes IP Address, Module Name and Locked Date/Time of the customer. To unblock the IP Address for a customer, click the ‘Lock’ icon for the corresponding customer under the ‘Action’ column.
